Why Become an Umpire?

Joining the Columbia Greene Umpires Association allows you to stay involved in baseball, give back to your community, and earn money while doing what you love. Flexible scheduling lets you work whenever you are available.

Flexible Schedule: Work games whenever you can and earn competitive pay.
Give Back to the Community: Support youth and high school baseball while maintaining fair play.
Stay Active Outdoors: Enjoy being outside, staying fit, and participating in the game you love.

Don't hesitate to reach out to any board members with questions about umpiring or to request a rule book.
