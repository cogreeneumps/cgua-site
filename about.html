Umpiring in Columbia & Greene Counties Since 1956

Our Mission

The Columbia Greene Umpires Association provides professional, qualified umpires for all levels of baseball in Columbia and Greene Counties. We are dedicated to maintaining the integrity of the game, ensuring fair play, and fostering a love of baseball in our communities.

Founded in 1956
Primarily serve 2 counties
70th anniversary in 2026
Cover all levels of baseball

What We Do

Professional Umpiring: Trained, certified umpires for youth leagues, high school, travel, and adult baseball.
Training & Development: Courses and clinics for new umpires and continuing education for experienced umpires.
Community Service: Giving back by ensuring fair play and helping young athletes love the game.
Rules & Standards: Staying current with official baseball rules to ensure consistent officiating.
