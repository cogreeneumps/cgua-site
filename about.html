Umpiring in Columbia & Greene Counties Since 1956

Our Mission

The Columbia Greene Umpires Association provides professional, qualified umpires for all levels of baseball in Columbia and Greene Counties. We are dedicated to maintaining the integrity of the game, ensuring fair play, and fostering a love of baseball in our communities.

Founded in 1956
Primarily serve 2 counties
70th anniversary in 2026
Cover all levels of baseball

What We Do

Professional Umpiring

We provide trained, certified umpires for youth leagues, high school games, travel baseball, and adult league baseball throughout Columbia & Greene Counties.

Training & Development

We offer comprehensive training courses and clinics for new umpires and continuing education to experienced umpires to maintain the highest level of quality.

Community Service

Our members are dedicated to giving back to the community by ensuring fair play and helping young athletes develop the love for the game.

Rules & Standards

We stay current with official baseball rules and regulations, ensuring consistent and accurate officiating across all the games we cover.

Join the Blue Team!

Are you ready to join the "blue" team on the diamond? We're always looking for dedicated individuals who want to be a part of our proud tradition on the baseball diamond. Whether you're an experienced umpire or just starting out, we want you to become part of the CGUA tradition!
