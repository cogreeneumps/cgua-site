Why Become a CGUA Umpire?

Be part of something great, stay active, and make money!

Joining the Columbia Greene Umpires Association gives you the opportunity to stay involved in baseball, earn money, and make a difference in your community — all while enjoying the outdoors and doing what you love.

Flexible Scheduling & Great Pay

Work whenever you can — your schedule is flexible! Most games pay very well, often cash, so you can make money while enjoying the sport.

Give Back to the Community

Help young athletes grow, support local baseball programs, and ensure fair play throughout Columbia & Greene Counties.

Stay Active & Outdoors

Spend your time on the diamond, enjoying the outdoors while staying physically active and engaged with the sport you love.

Join the Blue Team!

Are you ready to join the "blue" team on the diamond? Whether you're an experienced umpire or just starting out, we want you to become part of our proud CGUA tradition!
